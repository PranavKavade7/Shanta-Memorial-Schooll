About Us

Why We Are Better

Our school promotes diversity, inclusivity, and respect, preparing students to thrive in a global community.

How can I contact my child's teacher?

You can contact your child's teacher via email or by calling the school office to schedule a meeting.

What is the procedure for enrolling my child in your school?

To enroll your child, please visit our school office during regular business hours and bring the required documents such as proof of residency, birth certificate, and immunization records. You can also find enrollment forms on our website.

Does the school offer extracurricular activities?

Yes, we offer a variety of extracurricular activities including sports teams, clubs, and academic competitions. Please refer to our extracurricular activities Events page for more information.

How can I pay school fees?

School fees can be paid in person at the school office.
